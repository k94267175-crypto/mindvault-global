🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

Mind Vault Global collects information you voluntarily provide, including email addresses and project descriptions submitted through our Project Vault form. We also collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our forms is used solely to connect you with potential business partners and clients. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Analytics data is used only to understand website traffic and improve user experience.

3. AI Chat & Tools

Messages entered in our AI Chat Assistant are processed through Groq's API to generate responses. We do not store your chat conversations. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in the chat.

4. Cookies

We use Google Analytics cookies to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses third-party services including Google Analytics, Formspree (for form submissions), and Groq AI API. Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage users not to share sensitive personal or financial information through our platform.

7. Children's Privacy

Mind Vault Global is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: mindvaultglobal1@gmail.com
